Local Dishes

Meftune

The most famous specialty dish from Diyarbakir is Meftune which is made up of lamb meat and vegetable laced with garlic and sumac.

Kaburga Dolmasi

Another known dish is Kaburga Dolması which is a baked lamb's ribs stuffed with rice and many spices
